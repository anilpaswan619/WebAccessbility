A Research Study on Enhancing Accessibility and Inclusivity in User-Centric HCI

Welcome to our research study on enhancing accessibility and inclusivity in user-centric Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

Study Objectives

  • Explore the importance of accessibility in HCI
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of various accessibility tools
  • Identify best practices for designing inclusive user interfaces
  • Understand the challenges faced by users with disabilities
